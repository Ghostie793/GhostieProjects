Blue Mittins

by Ghostie Projects

These Blue Mittens are a much older pattern that I did. A good beginner project, it’s simple and easy to follow.

I found this project when I was looking for simple gift ideas. It was going into the colder seasons at the time, so I decided that it was perfect.

The person I was making the mittens for had smaller hands than the pattern was made for, so I had to adjust the pattern. This did cause the mittens to be a bit uneven in size, but it wasn’t too noticeable.

There also was a similar beanie pattern, and that was a joy to make as well. I'll make sure to mention this in another article.

Enjoy crafting!
