Star Headband

by Ghostie Projects

The Crochet Star Headband is a cute and easy pattern from Hi Crochet. It’s a simple pattern to follow with a beautiful outcome.
The stars are done in a hexagon, starting in a base of six. They are pretty easy themselves, and fun to repeat. The only difficulty I had with the stars was remembering to link them together when crocheting.

Another slight difficulty was the end bit of the chain. It was hard to get them even on both sides, and to use the same stitches.
Overall, this was a fun, quick, and light crochet pattern that was a cute product. It’s great for a bit of sparkle or fun in an outfit, or as a special gift for someone. Enjoy crafting!
